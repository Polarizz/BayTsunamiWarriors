For those unfamiliar with the sport, dragon boating is a boat of 20 paddlers, a drummer, and a steers-person, paddling to cross a finish line faster than their competition. Dragon boat is a team sport that emphasizes elements of power, speed, synchronization and endurance. The wooden boats are long and narrow with painted scales and a decorative head and tail, made to resemble a dragon.
